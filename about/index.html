Bio

Traitor of mathematics, I have been working as a data scientist since 2018.

I come from Taiwan and was once a mathematics student at universities in Taipei and in Paris for several years, before diverging and becoming an computer engineer.

One primary objective and interest of mine is the search of utility of mathematics.

By switching my itinerary to the computer science land, I wish to learn and contribute to more useful technologies, especially those which utilise university-level mathematics and beyond.

The collection of this kind of useful mathematics, I wish, could be shared on this website to my dear family and friends.
